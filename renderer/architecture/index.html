Interactive architecture

Architecture

Explore the model from system flow to implementation detail.

    Select a block or connection to inspect it. Open a detail node to move deeper. Drag the canvas to follow long flows. Pinch with two fingers on a touchscreen, or use a two-finger trackpad scroll or mouse wheel, to zoom around the gesture. Keyboard shortcuts: press Z to zoom in on the selected block, Shift+Z to fit the whole board, and J or K to step to a parent or child of the selection; when a step has several candidates, J and K move the highlight and Enter confirms. Otherwise, Enter opens the selected block's detail board and Escape returns to its parent.
